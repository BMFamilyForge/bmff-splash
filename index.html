About Blue Mountain Family Forge

Our Story

As parents raising kids right here in the Walla Walla Valley, we know the exact struggle of trying to find a weekend activity that keeps everyone happy. We realized our community needed a space that bridges the gap—a place where younger children can burn off energy, older kids have next-level attractions to graduate into, and parents actually have a great time too.

Our Mission

To forge unforgettable family memories by providing a safe, clean, and thrilling entertainment hub. From an expansive indoor soft play area to high-energy arcades and immersive escape rooms, we are building a multi-generational playground. We believe parents shouldn't have to compromise on comfort, which is why our cafe and bar will offer a premium space to relax, connect, and recharge while the kids play.

Our Vision

Blue Mountain Family Forge isn't just a business; it's a community investment. We are building the ultimate year-round destination for epic birthday parties, weekend adventures, and family nights out. This is the venue our community has been waiting for, and we are incredibly proud to bring it to life.
