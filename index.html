Blue Mountain Family Forge

COMING SOON TO WALLA WALLA, WA

Our Story

If you are raising kids right here in the Walla Walla Valley, you already know the struggle of finding a year-round, indoor activity that keeps everyone happy—especially when the weather turns cold. As a local dad, I got tired of the "winter weekend gap" where toddlers have a few great options, but older kids and teenagers are left with nowhere to go. I realized our community deserved better. So, I decided to build the solution.

Blue Mountain Family Forge is a massive, multi-generational entertainment hub designed for the entire family. We are transforming the standard indoor playground concept into a premium destination where kids can burn off energy, teenagers can hang out, and parents can actually relax.

What's Inside The Forge?

  • 🧗‍♂️ The Soft Play Forge: A massive, multi-level indoor playground structure featuring tube slides, dynamic climbers, and a dedicated, safe toddler zone.
  • 🕹️ The Arcade: A state-of-the-art, high-energy arcade featuring both redemption ticket games and classic cabinet play.
  • 🗝️ The Escape Rooms: Highly immersive, high-tech escape rooms uniquely engineered to challenge teenagers, college students, and adults.
  • 🍕 The Blue Mountain Cafe & Bar: A high-end food and beverage space featuring fresh food, craft drinks, and comfortable seating with clear sightlines to the play areas, so parents can genuinely recharge.

Current Status

I am currently in the final stages of commercial lease negotiations for a massive facility in the Eastgate area. Because I want to ensure the building—and its floorplan—is absolutely perfect for our community, the exact address and final square footage are being kept under wraps until the ink is dry on the contract.
