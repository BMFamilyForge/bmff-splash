Blue Mountain Family Forge

COMING SOON TO WALLA WALLA, WA

OUR VISION

If you are raising kids right here in the Walla Walla Valley, you know the struggle of finding a year-round, indoor activity that keeps everyone happy. As a local dad, I realized our community deserved more than just a place to play—it deserved a place where **everyone** belongs.

Blue Mountain Family Forge is a 16,000 sq. ft. community-driven entertainment hub. We are filling the gap in Walla Walla by creating a "Third Place" that serves our families, our local businesses, and our neighbors with diverse needs.

What's Inside The Forge?

🎡 The Soft Play Forge

A massive, multi-level inclusive playground. Beyond daily play, we host Weekly Sensory-Friendly Hours (reduced noise/lights) and Adaptive Inclusion Sessions specifically designed for adults with developmental disabilities.

🗝️ The Escape Rooms

High-tech, self-restarting rooms including Wonderland and the 18+ Cabin in the Woods. Perfect for family adventures, college date nights, and Professional Team Building for local Walla Walla businesses.

🍕 The Blue Mountain Cafe & Bar

A high-end "Parent's Oasis" serving craft beer, wine, and fresh food. Relax and recharge with clear sightlines to the play area, or drop the kids off for our weekly 4-Hour Parent Date Night program.

🛡️ The RFID Safety System

Our facility is 100% cashless and account-based. Your RFID wristband ensures secure entry/exit tracking for your kids and lightning-fast transactions, so you spend less time in line and more time at play.
