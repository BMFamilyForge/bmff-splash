COMING SOON TO WALLA WALLA, WA

Our Vision for an Inclusive Walla Walla

Walla Walla is built on community, but for many of our neighbors, there are still significant gaps in where they can go to feel truly welcome. As a local dad, I realized our community deserved more than just a place to play—it deserved a "Third Place" where every resident, regardless of age or ability, has a seat at the table.

01. Closing the Inclusivity Gap

Our city has a vibrant population, yet two specific groups are often left without dedicated spaces for recreation. We are committed to changing that:

  • Adults with Mental Disabilities: We host dedicated weekly sessions where adults with developmental or mental disabilities can enjoy our multi-level play structures in a dignified, supportive, and age-appropriate atmosphere.
  • Sensory-Friendly Hours: Once a week, we "turn down the volume." We dim the lights and silence the arcade cabinets to provide a calm, low-stimulation environment for children and adults with sensory sensitivities.

02. Strengthening the Local Workforce

The Forge serves as a premier hub for the Walla Walla business community, from downtown banks to local wineries.

  • Team Building: Our automated escape rooms, including Wonderland and the 18+ Cabin in the Woods, offer a modern way for teams to foster communication and problem-solving.
  • Facility Rentals: Our space is available for company functions, holiday events, and training sessions, complete with professional catering and a comfortable bar environment.

03. The "Parenting Gap" Solution

We believe parents shouldn't have to "endure" a play center; they should enjoy it.

  • The Parent Oasis: Our integrated bar and cafe offer a high-end space for adults to relax with a beer or wine while children play within clear sightlines.
  • Parent Drop-Off Program: Once a week, we offer a 4-hour supervised drop-off window. Parents can enjoy a date night downtown while kids play in a secure, RFID-monitored environment.

04. Modern Safety & Access

We utilize a frictionless, RFID-account based system for safety and speed. While we are a cashless facility, we remain accessible to all by accepting cash at the front desk to be loaded onto guest wristbands, ensuring every member of our community can participate.
